Connect Google Sheet

This app stores products, sales and expenses in a Google Sheet through a Google Apps Script web app.

Quick Setup

  1. Create a blank Google Sheet for your store.
  2. Open Extensions > Apps Script and paste the supplied apps-script/Code.gs file.
  3. Run setupSheets once and authorize access.
  4. Deploy as a Web app, executing as you, accessible to anyone with the link.
  5. Paste its deployment URL here and connect.

Keep the deployment URL private. Anyone with the URL can send transactions to the sheet.

Ready for sales.
